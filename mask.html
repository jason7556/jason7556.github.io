MASK

Projector will project random videos on the pliable textile when sounds that made by spectators exceed the set decibels.

The face will gradually been pushed out on the screen, depending on the distance between the viewer and the installation.

-

Sometimes I like to be solitary, but in the meanwhile, I am confined in some environment that I have to interact with others. Then I must bring out a well-trained and sophisticated me to face people. Utilizing hollow eyes and fluently speaking mouth to communicate with others, just like a constantly operating machine.
